Their tales are a grim exploration of loyalty and betrayal, the complex motives behind their actions, and the heavy price of their choices. Their words offer a deep insight into the nature of betrayal, the pain it causes, and the eternal regret that follows.

The sight is horrifying yet mesmerizing, the ultimate punishment for the ultimate betrayal. The air is filled with the sound of Lucifer's gnashing teeth and the muffled screams of his victims. This is the deepest point of Hell, a place of despair and hopelessness, where the weight of eternal punishment is most acutely felt.
